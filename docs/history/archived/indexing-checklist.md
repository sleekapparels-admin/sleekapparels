## üîß TROUBLESHOOTING:

### **Issue: Pages Not Indexing After 7 Days**
**Solution:**
1. Check Coverage report for specific errors
2. Re-request indexing manually
3. Verify pages are crawlable (no robots.txt blocking)
4. Check for duplicate content issues

### **Issue: "Crawled - currently not indexed"**
**This is normal!** Google crawled but hasn't indexed yet.
- Wait 3-7 more days
- Request indexing again
- Ensure content is unique & high-quality (‚úÖ you have 98,000 words!)

### **Issue: "Discovered - currently not indexed"**
**Solution:**
- Google found your page via sitemap but hasn't crawled yet
- Be patient (can take 1-2 weeks)
- Request indexing manually to speed up
