### **Step 2: Google Search Console Setup** (10 minutes) âš¡ **CRITICAL**

#### **A. Add Your Property:**
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click **"+ Add property"**

## ðŸš¨ COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

### **Issue 1: Pages Not Indexed After 7 Days**
**Solution:**
1. Check GSC Coverage report for errors
2. Verify sitemap.xml is submitted
3. Request indexing manually again
4. Ensure robots.txt allows indexing
5. Check if pages have `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` (should be removed)

---

### **Issue 2: Low CTR (<1%)**
**Solution:**
1. Rewrite meta descriptions to be more compelling
2. Add emotional triggers ("Low MOQ", "Fast Turnaround", "OEKO-TEX")
3. Include pricing range in description
4. Add year to title tag ("2025 Guide")

---

### **Issue 3: High Impressions, Low Clicks**
**Solution:**
- Your pages are ranking but not compelling users to click
- Fix title tags to be more specific
- Add rich snippets (we already have FAQ schema)
- Consider adding pricing in title ("$3.50-$18/unit")

---

### **Issue 4: Zero Traffic After 30 Days**
**Solution:**
1. Check if Google has indexed pages (use `site:yourdomain.com` in Google)
2. Verify GSC shows no crawl errors
3. Ensure pages have unique, high-quality content (we have 98,000 words)
4. Request indexing again
5. Build 3-5 backlinks from industry sites
